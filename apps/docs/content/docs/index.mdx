Welcome to the OpenCoop documentation. Here you'll find everything you need to get started with managing your cooperative's shareholders, shares, transactions, and dividends.

## What is OpenCoop?

OpenCoop is a multi-tenant SaaS platform that helps cooperatives manage their entire shareholding lifecycle — from shareholder registration to dividend distribution.

## Key Features

- **Shareholder Management** — Register individuals, companies, and minors. Track statuses, contact details, and beneficial owners.
- **Share Classes** — Define multiple share classes with different prices and rules.
- **Transactions** — Process purchases, sales, and transfers with automatic payment tracking.
- **Dividends** — Calculate and distribute dividends with Belgian withholding tax support.
- **Documents** — Generate share certificates, transaction confirmations, and tax documents as PDF.
- **Bank Import** — Import CODA/CSV bank statements and automatically match payments to transactions.
